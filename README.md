# Spuddenly-Human
A venture by a non-coder into the realm of video game development

A narrative comedy game about a potato trying to understand human emotions through household disasters.

Built in Godot.

---

## Premise

One day, a potato becomes aware.

Unfortunately, it also becomes curious about humans.

The potato explores a small apartment room by room, studying emotional behaviour the way a confused scientist might study unstable wildlife.

Why are humans angry at toasters?

Why does one wrong text message cause psychic collapse?

Why do people refuse to admit obvious mistakes?

The potato intends to find out.

---

## Current Demo

### Kitchen: Frustration
The toaster appears broken.

The actual problem:
It is not plugged in.

The potato learns that humans become deeply upset when reality fails to match expectation, especially when the solution was embarrassingly simple.

### Living Room: Embarrassment
A human sends the wrong message to the apartment group chat.

The potato investigates the emotional fallout and learns that embarrassment is what happens when private failure escapes into public space.

In progress.
